Arcade Mission

Built like a playable cabinet, published like a modern web project.

This tribute takes the spirit of classic Arkanoid and rebuilds it as a browser-ready arcade experience with a title video, soundtrack rotation, enemy interference, boss fights, and polished cabinet presentation.

Read the Project Story

Core Features

  • Three handcrafted rounds plus a Doh-inspired final encounter
  • Attract mode, title video, arcade HUD, and seeded run support
  • Enemy fly-ins, capsules, multiball, catch, laser, and extra lives
  • Mouse and keyboard support with browser-friendly music playback

Power-Ups

  • E Expand the paddle
  • S Slow the ball
  • C Catch and hold the ball
  • L Enable laser fire
  • D Launch multiball
  • P Gain an extra life
